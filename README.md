# The Move

**A self-motivation tool for men to overcome the fear of approaching women.**

---

## What It Is

*The Move* is a single-page motivational web app designed to reframe the fear of approaching someone you are attracted to. Instead of minimizing the fear of rejection, it replaces it with a more powerful one: the fear of a life spent alone because you never tried.

The core insight is simple. Not approaching is not a neutral act. It is a choice with real, compounding consequences. This page makes those consequences visceral and personal.

---

## Features

- **Personalized experience** - enter your birth year and every age, year, and scene card is calculated specifically for you
- **Scenes from a life unlived** - vivid age-specific snapshots of what chronic inaction looks like at 32, 41, 55, and 70
- **She will not approach** - explains why waiting for her to make the first move is not a strategy
- **She is waiting too** - your future partner exists right now and is hoping someone like you walks up
- **Cost comparison table** - side-by-side breakdown of the real cost of silence vs action
- **Live clock** - ticking in real time as a reminder that life is passing
- **Health statistics** - science-backed consequences of chronic loneliness
- **5-second countdown** - a final push with a phrase on each count, ending with GO

---

## How to Use

Open `index.html` in any browser. Enter your birth year when prompted. Read from top to bottom. Hit the countdown button when you are ready to go.

Designed to be read **before going out** - on your phone, right before you step outside.

## Tech Stack

- Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
- Zero dependencies, zero frameworks, zero build steps
- Google Fonts (Bebas Neue + Lora) via CDN
- Single file - the entire app lives in `index.html`

## Deployment

### GitHub Pages (recommended)

1. Fork or clone this repository
2. Rename `the_move.html` to `index.html`
3. Go to **Settings** then **Pages**
4. Set source to **main branch / root**
5. Your site will be live at `https://your-username.github.io/the-move`

### Any static host

Upload `index.html` to Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, or any basic web server. No backend or server-side processing required.

## Design Decisions

Dark, high-contrast aesthetic built around urgency and emotional weight. **Bebas Neue** for display headings, **Lora** italic for body text — balancing intensity with readability. Near-black backgrounds with red accents. Deliberately uncomfortable. Deliberately serious.

Scroll-triggered animations reveal content progressively so the emotional arc builds as you read rather than overwhelming all at once.

The personalization engine calculates your specific future years, ages, and scene card ages from your birth year — making the content feel real rather than abstract.

---

## Philosophy

Most confidence advice focuses on making rejection feel smaller. This tool takes the opposite approach. It makes inaction feel bigger. The goal is not to eliminate nervousness. The goal is to make the alternative more frightening than the approach.

> *Rejection fades in hours. Regret lasts a lifetime.*

The tool is built around one core truth: women rarely make the first move. Not because they are not interested, but because social conditioning means the move almost always falls to the man. If he does not act, nobody acts. The moment passes. Permanently.
